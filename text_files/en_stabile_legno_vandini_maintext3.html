The technologyX-Lam(also known asCLT, from EnglishCrossesLaminatedTimber) represents one of the most significant innovations in the field of sustainable construction. It is a construction system based on cross-laminated solid wood laminated panels.

How an X-panel is madeLam?

An X-panelLamit is composed of several layers (usually 3, 5 or 7) of conifer wood boards (often fir), dried and overlapped so that the grain of each layer isrotated 90°compared to the adjacent one. The layers are then glued together under strong pressure.

This "crossed" configuration gives the material exceptional properties:

  • Dimensional stability:The variations in the wood due to humidity are canceled out by the orthogonal arrangement of the layers.

  • Mechanical resistance:The panel can withstand very high loads, behaving like a rigid "slab", similar to reinforced concrete but much lighter.
