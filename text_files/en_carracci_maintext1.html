Casa Carracci in Bologna: Historical and Logistical Analysis

The address Via San Carlo, 19 in Bologna is associated with a property historically named "Casa Carracci" (currently also known as "San Carlo Holiday House" and used as tourist accommodation).

The building, protected by the Superintendence of Cultural Heritage, is identified by local tradition as one of the places where the painter Ludovico Carracci (Bologna, 1555 – Bologna, 1619) lived and worked, as well as one of the sites of the famous workshop of the three Carracci cousins (Ludovico, Agostino, and Annibale), founders in 1582 of the Accademia dei Desiderosi (later known as Accademia degli Incamminati).
