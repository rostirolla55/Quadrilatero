There used to be an alley in Bologna called Pugliole di San Bernardino . This street disappeared following the urban planning interventions of the 1930s that led to the creation of Via Guglielmo Marconi (then Via Roma).

From the Napoleonic and Papal State land registry of 1871, we have a map showing the alleyway that connects the current number 25 of Via Polese with the current Piazza dei Martiri. The Reno tunnel, located at the level of Via Marconi 51, corresponds to the alleyway.
