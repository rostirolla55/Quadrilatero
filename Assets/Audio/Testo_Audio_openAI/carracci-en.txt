Welcome...
Today we immerse ourselves in the great history of Bolognese art... walking in the very places where a true visual revolution began.

We are in the heart of the ancient Poggiale district. It is in these streets... between via San Carlo and via Riva di Reno... that the Carracci family had its roots.

Here the cousins Ludovico... Agostino... and Annibale Carracci were born and lived... the masters who at the end of the sixteenth century forever changed the course of European painting.

Think of their workshop... a vibrant environment where they abandoned the rigidities of late Mannerism... to rediscover drawing from life... nature... and the truth of emotions.

Walking in this area today... we can almost imagine the excitement of those young artists... who observed the faces of common people and the light of the city... to bring them onto grand canvases.

An indissoluble bond between artistic genius and the urban space of Bologna.

One step at a time.
