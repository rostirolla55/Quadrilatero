The Basilica of Santa Maria Maggiore , located at Via Galliera 10, is considered the oldest church in Bologna dedicated to the Madonna. It is a place of great charm, combining early Christian origins with an imposing Baroque appearance.

Here is the main historical and artistic information:

History and Architecture

  • Very ancient origins: Tradition and some papal bulls (such as that of Gregory VII) suggest that the church already existed in the 5th or 6th century . It initially stood on the Cardo Maximus of the Roman city.

  • Renovations: It was rebuilt in Romanesque style in the 12th century (consecrated in 1187) and became the seat of a Collegiate Church of Canons.

  • The current appearance: The face we see today is the result of the 1665 transformation carried out by the architect Paolo Canali , who replaced the old wooden truss ceiling with a baroque barrel vault.

  • The façade: It is characterised by a three-arched portico. The upper part, with the triangular pediment, was completed only recently, in 1955 .

  • Recent restorations: Severely damaged by the 2012 earthquake, the basilica remained closed for years. After significant structural consolidation and fresco restoration, it reopened for worship in November 2019 .

Notable works of art

The interior, with three naves, is a true gallery of Bolognese painting:

  • The Vault: Decorated between 1936 and 1938 by the Valtellina painter Eliseo Fumagalli , with a perspective effect that simulates an open sky populated by angels.

  • Paintings: It houses canvases by masters such as Orazio Samacchini , Prospero Fontana , Alessandro Tiarini and Vincenzo Spisanelli .

  • Chapel of the Blessed Sacrament: Notable for the stucco decorations by Angelo Gabriello Piò , a famous sculptor of eighteenth-century Bologna.

  • Altarpiece of the Assumption: Begun by Gio. Francesco Bezzi (il Nosadella ) and completed by Prospero Fontana (in the choir).

  • Paintings: Works by Alessandro Tiarini , Orazio Samacchini , Vincenzo Spisanelli , Mauro Gandolfi and Alessandro Guardassoni .

  • Fico Crucifix: An ancient wooden crucifix that tradition dates back to before the year 1000.

  • Decorations: The vault was decorated in the modern era (1936-1938) by the painter Eliseo Fumagalli, while the Chapel of the Holy Sacrament boasts precious stuccoes by Angelo Gabriello Piò .

  • Interesting fact: Inside, an ancient stone cross is preserved, discovered during excavations, which testifies to the ancient sacredness of the place.
